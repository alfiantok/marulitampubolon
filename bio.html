About Me

Maruli Ario Tampubolon, popularly known as Maruli Tampubolon, is an Indonesian Batak descendant who profoundly possess passionate talents in his utmost unique abilities in the realm of intellectualism and entertainment. He Sings, Acts, and also is an advocate and counsellor at law who was born in Jakarta on the 26th of March 1987. His formal educational background was beautifully decorated with three degrees from Sarjana Hukum (SH), Bachelor of Business majoring in finance and business law, and a Master of Business Administration. In Juxtaposition, he found himself trapped by his own enigma of self justification upon his musical endeavor. Thus, his eagerness in striving more to pursue his dream by becoming better than his yesterday’s in refining his God given talents is the fundamental personification of his indomitable characteristic.

Maruli Tampubolon was born into a lawyer family on March, 26th 1987 in Jakarta, Indonesia. His educational background was all correlated with law and finance. He has three formal educational degrees; A Bachelor of Business, Double Major in Finance and Business Law and a Master of Business Administration from Edith Cowan University in Western Australia. He also took a Law Degree in which he finished his Sarjana Hukum from Universitas Jakarta in Indonesia. He began his career as a lawyer both in litigation and corporate matters as he had successfully obtained his Bar License as an Advocate in Indonesia. Somehow he decided to make his dream come true by taking his singing and acting hobbies become professional. “Because music and/or movie never fail to bring some magical vibe as an ornament of happiness to endless souls” he said.

Career

My Career is the personification of my passionate talents being expresse in a formation that people acknowledge and/or understand and/or comprehend it as professions.So, when it comes to an inquiry about a career, it is surely a perpetual work in progress. I have my unique ways of actualizing my abilities in the realm of intellectualism and entertainment. So, please allow me to reiterate my brief elucidation.My formal educational background was beautifully decorated with three degrees ranging from Sarjana Hukum (SH), Bachelor of Business Double Majors in Business Law and Finance and a Master of Business Administration. Ergo, with a high confidence interval I can legitimately state that I am an Advocate and Counselor at Law. Notwithstanding, I am also a very passionate singer and actor. In juxtaposition, it is conspicuous for me to say that I am the medium of blessings by advocating passionate exploration of talents through the endless cosmic of arts.” - Maruli Tampubolon

He began his life as a singer in 2014 by choosing Syahrini to be his duet partner for their single “Cinta Sendiri” and then coproduced and wrote an EDM song with Wingky Wiryawan titled “Love Comes Around”. After that he remake one of the most popular song from Vina Panduwinata which was transformatively rearranged by Andi Rianto, the legendary song “September Ceria” in 2015, then released his own song “Ruang” at the same year. One year after that, Marulicollaborated with one of the most famous Indonesian female singer Raisa in the Movie “Terjebak Nostalgia”. The title of the movie itself was taken from one of Raisa’s song. In that project, Maruli did not only act with Raisa, but also wrote and produced the song “Butterfly” which was composed and arranged by Aldi Nada Permana. After releasing another song of his “Kau UcapSelamat Tinggal”, as of now he is preparing a long awaited musical album which shall be released by the end of September 2018.

Before starring in “Terjebak Nostalgia” which genre was considered a romantic movie, Maruli was energetically played a role as a sergeant in a Famous Military Action Web- series titled “Patriot” which was produced by NET TV. Furthermore, he continuously tried to be more versatile by playing a role as an n obstetriciangynecologist in a drama- comedy movie by Angga Sasongko “Bukaan 8” and ultimately challenged himself in the toughest role yet as a Captain of Indonesia’s finest counter terrorist unit 81 on an inspired based Hostage Rescue Mission from The Special Force, Kopassus, in a National Military Endorsed Action movie involving full scale presentation of Indonesia’s finest Instrumental Weapons System and hundreds of elite soldiers from Army, Navy and Air forces which was directed by Mirwan Suwarso “Merah PutihMemanggil” in 2017.
